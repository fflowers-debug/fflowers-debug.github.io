Our story: More than just a coffee

What started as a way to make someones day turned into a full coffee experience. Beginning with a simple game of guessing the color inside my coffee mug. I quickly noticed that it felt like there was a little extra caffeine in my coffee on days that I guessed right." Now, our coffee shop brings this fun experience to life, offering moments of connection and excitement with every cup.
