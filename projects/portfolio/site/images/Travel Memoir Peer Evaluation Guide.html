Please answer and elaborate on the following questions.

-1- In your opinion, does the memoir begin in the best place, at the best time, in the best way? Do any of the opening sentences seem awkward or forced?

-2- Does the author engage the reader throughout the memoir? What suggestions can you offer to help the author in this regard?

-3- How is nostalgia working in the memoir? Is it effective?

-4- Is there a sense of estrangement and renewal in the memoir? Any suggestions on how to incorporate and develop these themes?

-5- Is it easy to follow the memoir? Is there enough support where it may be needed?

-6- Is the structure and coherence of the memoir effective and appropriate for this audience?

-7- Does the author employ the right mixture and balance of ethos, logos and pathos?

-8- Is the author effective at bringing the place to life? What suggestions can you offer to improve the descriptive elements?

-9- Does the memoir read well? At what point do you think the energy reaches its height? Slips away or falls flat? Do you have questions that need to be answered?

-10- Is the tone, voice, style, and substance of the memoir effective? How can it be improved?

-11- Do you think the author discovers and shows enough to be compelling? Is there enough support? Is it believable?

-12- Does the memoir end at the best point in the best way? How can the author improve the conclusion?

-13- Does the title work for the style and content of the memoir? Can you suggest something else?

-14- Is the language active? Are there instances of passive voice?

-15- How are the mechanics? (Address spelling, grammar, run-on sentences and other sentence-level issues that you identify.)

-16- Do you think the memoir succeeds in what it set out to do? Can you make any suggestions to help the writer?
